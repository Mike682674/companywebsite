Job Description:

Infrastructure Technician

Location: Remote (Supporting New York, NY Client)

Schedule: Monday to Friday, 6:00 AM – 3:00 PM (Philippine Time)

Salary: Above Average

Overview

We are looking for a highly skilled and adaptable Infrastructure Technician to join our remote team and support a New York-based client. This role requires expertise in managing and maintaining IT infrastructure, ensuring seamless operations, and addressing technical issues promptly. As an Infrastructure Technician, you will play a crucial role in providing efficient technical support and ensuring the stability of our client’s systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform remote management and maintenance of IT infrastructure, including networks, servers, and hardware.
  • Monitor system performance and ensure optimal functioning of the client’s IT environment.
  • Diagnose and troubleshoot infrastructure issues to minimize downtime and disruptions.
  • Install, configure, and manage systems such as structured cabling, switches, routers, and network devices.
  • Provide support for data centers, CCTV, access control systems, and fiber GPON infrastructure.
  • Ensure compliance with client-specific protocols, safety standards, and best practices.
  • Collaborate with the client’s on-site team and vendors to resolve hardware and connectivity issues.
  • Document procedures, configurations, and troubleshooting steps for future reference.
  • Regularly report to the client on infrastructure status, incident resolution, and system improvements.
  • Provide proactive recommendations to enhance infrastructure performance and reliability.

Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Information Technology, Computer Engineering, Electronics, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in IT infrastructure management, support, and troubleshooting.
  • Expertise in structured cabling, fiber GPON, switches, and network systems.
  • Familiarity with CCTV, access control systems (DACS), and other security solutions is an advantage.
  • Strong knowledge of system configuration, backup, and disaster recovery.
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Familiarity with remote monitoring and management tools is a plus.
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines in a remote setting.

Why Join Us?

  • Competitive above-average salary.
  • Opportunities to enhance your skills by supporting a New York-based client.
  • Work in a dynamic and collaborative virtual environment.
  • Be part of a team that values innovation and excellence in IT solutions.

If you have the technical expertise and dedication to excel in infrastructure management, we’d love to have you on board. Join us in delivering top-notch IT solutions while working remotely in a flexible and growth-focused role!
