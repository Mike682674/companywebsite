Welcome to House of IT

15 Years+ of IT Experience

Over 15 years ago, House of IT started as an Aussie-owned company operating in the Philippines. House of IT has had the pleasure of serving clients across the globe with major partners situated in the US, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK.

Whether your IT needs are small-scale or large-scale, leave it to us to expertly design, implement and manage your infrastructures 24/7. Our philosophy on IT is simple… put you the customer first. For this reason, we only work with competent, professional consultants and reliable suppliers to deliver exceptional results.

We are a team of highly skilled professionals with a wealth of experience in the IT industry. Our team is dedicated to providing top-notch services and solutions to meet your needs. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our ability to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Quality Management System

House of IT believes quality is vital when choosing your IT infrastructures. We do not compromise quality in designing, implementing, or management of your IT infrastructure.

There are seven (7) principles of quality management that any company must incorporate.

● Engagement of people

● Customer focus,

● Leadership

● Process approach,

● improvement,

● evidence-based decision making

● Relationship Management

We are ISO certified to make sure our clients receive top-quality management. The ISMS ISO 27001:2013 is an international stamp of approval in regards to our ability to protect customers’ information. Similarly, the QMS ISO 9001:2015 recognizes our compliance with international standards in furthering customer satisfaction and strong performance. We identify with all the principles of the ISO certificate.

Based on customer feedback, our management team seeks to motivate employees by emphasizing flawless quality when designing, implementing, and managing IT infrastructure. Every House of IT employee, from contractors to engineers, is adequately trained to perform their duties.

Employees, stakeholders, suppliers, investors, and the community at large are integral to our decision-making process. At House of IT, informed IT decisions are based on accurate and reliable data sources that promote the integrity of your business. With growth as one of our core values, we constantly seek areas of improvement for our clients and ourselves. For this reason, we monitor the activities of your business and find ways to help you expand via information technology.
