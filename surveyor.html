Job Description:

Surveyor (Electrical, CCTV, DACS, Structured Cabling, Fiber GPON)

Location: Philippines

Salary: Above Average

Overview

We are seeking a skilled and detail-oriented Surveyor to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for conducting site surveys, preparing project estimates, and ensuring the successful implementation of electrical systems, CCTV, DACS, structured cabling, and fiber GPON installations. This role requires a proactive individual with strong technical expertise and the ability to collaborate effectively with project teams and clients.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct site surveys to assess project requirements for electrical systems, CCTV, DACS, structured cabling, and fiber GPON installations.
  • Prepare detailed project plans, including timelines, manpower requirements, and material estimates.
  • Collaborate with engineers, project managers, and clients to ensure accurate project specifications and deliverables.
  • Identify potential challenges during site surveys and propose effective solutions.
  • Ensure compliance with local regulations, safety standards, and company policies.
  • Document survey findings and prepare comprehensive reports for project planning and execution.
  • Coordinate with the local government unit (LGU) and other stakeholders as needed for project approvals and permits.
  • Monitor project progress and provide updates to the management team.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree or vocational training in Electrical Engineering, Electronics, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in surveying for electrical systems, CCTV, DACS, structured cabling, and fiber GPON projects.
  • Strong knowledge of project estimation, design, and layout planning.
  • Familiarity with AutoCAD and other relevant software is an advantage.
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Willingness to travel to project sites across the Philippines.

Why Join Us?

  • Competitive above-average salary.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • Be part of a dynamic team dedicated to delivering innovative solutions.
