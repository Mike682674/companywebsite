COMPREHENSIVE SECURITY SYSTEM

As a business, you naturally deal with company and customer's sensitive information that must be protected from the general public and should only be accessed by authorized personnel.

Access control systems allow you to control who has access to your business facilities and how they access it. An enterprise with multiple sites and employees needs multiple security access levels to protect sensitive company information from intruders.

WHAT IS AN ACCESS CONTROL SYSTEM?

Access control systems perform the identification, authentication, and authorization of users. This system gives you the ability to guard against external and unpredicted internal attacks.

Key Features

Biometric Authentication

Verify the identity of individuals accessing your building via biometric data.

Swipe Cards

Limit access to physical locations by only allowing those with key card access to enter.

Personalized Support

Have a unique security need? Let us know what personalized solution you need for your business so we can help

BENEFITS OF ACCESS CONTROL SYSTEM

Guaranteed Security

Reduce the incidents of thefts and accidents by monitoring the movement of individuals in the building.

Accountability

Every entry and exit is recorded so you know who is where and when.

Secure Sensitive Information

Decide who has access to your proprietary information and your trade secrets by limiting access to specific areas

Controlled Access

Hard to duplicate. Once an employee leaves the company just remove access.

After Hours Support

An access control system organizes the chaotic task of keeping track of employees during swing and graveyard shifts.
